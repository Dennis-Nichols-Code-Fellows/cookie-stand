Our Story

Fishing for Chinook Salmon in the beautiful rivers of Washington State and the Pacific Northwest is a family tradition. One of the ways we've always escaped the business of the city has been to take a trip to the peaceful environs of the Nisqually River. However, the sad truth is that the river we grew up with is not the same one we are taking our children to. We started The Salmon Cookie Store to draw attention to the challenges facing our rivers and the beautiful creatures which call them home.
